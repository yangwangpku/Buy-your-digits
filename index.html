Game Objective

    The aim is to correctly guess the total occurrences of a specific digit (1-4) present in both players' hands.

Setup

  1. Each player starts with a random sequence of 5 digits ranging from 1-4.
  2. Players take turns to make a guess or challenge the opponent's previous guess.

Gameplay

  1. Making a Guess:

    On your turn, you can guess the total occurrences of a specific digit. Your guess must be more aggressive than the previous one. (e.g., after a guess of (3 3s), valid guesses include (3 4s), (5 1s), or (6 2s), but not (3 2s) or (2 4s)).

  2. Challenging a Guess:

    Instead of guessing, you can challenge the opponent's last guess. A challenge ends the round and all digits are revealed.

Determining the Round Winner

  1. If the guess is correct or underestimated, the challenger loses the round.
  2. If the guess is overestimated, the guesser loses the round.

Post-Round

  1. The round loser loses one digit.
  2. A new round begins with the remaining digits.
  3. The loser starts the next round.

Winning the Game

    The game continues until one player loses all their digits. The player who still has digits remaining is the winner.
